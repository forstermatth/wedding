Recommendations

Travel

  • We recommend a flight to Nanimo (Vancouver Island)
  • Renting a car in Nanimo to drive to Ucluelet is a great way to see some awesome landscape on Vancouver Island
  • If you choose to drive through Vancouver, be aware of; ferry schedule changes, line ups, and rush hour traffic - all which can cause very long delays.

What to bring

  • Hiking boots! (or at least good shoes)
  • Rain gear - coat at the least
  • A camera - the scenery is incredible
  • A game to play after supper! - We are not dancing, so instead we invite our guests to bring their favorite table top game.
